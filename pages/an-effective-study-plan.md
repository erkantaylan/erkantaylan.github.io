An effective study plan comes from four parts.
First of all, to study effective our body should be healthy, when you are sick you might try to study but it won't be effective. Secondly, you should avoid distractions, such as if your home/work area is too noisy, you should consider going to a library which is closest to your home or work. Another one is that you need to forget everything but your study. Worst distraction is your mind, your bills, or girlfriend or your job or your school. Last but not least make your study room as empty as possible. Extra objects might distract you.
